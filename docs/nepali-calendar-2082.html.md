## Nepal Fiscal Year in 2082

Nepal's fiscal year runs from Shrawan 1 to Ashadh end (approximately mid-July to mid-July of the following year). Within BS 2082:
- **FY 2081/82:** Shrawan 1, 2081 to Ashadh 31, 2082 - ended approximately mid-July 2025.
- **FY 2082/83:** Shrawan 1, 2082 to Ashadh 31, 2083 - began approximately mid-July 2025, running through mid-July 2026.

All tax filings, government procurement, budget allocations, and audits in Nepal use the fiscal year notation (e.g., "FY 2082/83"). ## FAQ - Nepali Calendar 2082

**When did Nepali year 2082 start and end?**
BS 2082 started on April 14, 2025 AD (Baishakh 1, 2082) and ended on April 13, 2026 AD (Chaitra 30, 2082).

**What year was 2025 in Nepali calendar?**
January 1–April 13, 2025 was in BS 2081. April 14, 2025 onward was in BS 2082. So most of 2025 AD was the Nepali year 2082.

**When was Nepali New Year 2082?**
Nepali New Year 2082 (Nava Barsha नव वर्ष) was on April 14, 2025 AD - Baishakh 1, 2082 BS. It is a national holiday in Nepal.

**What were the major festivals in BS 2082?**
Dashain (October 2025), Tihar/Deepawali (October 2025), Chhath Puja (October–November 2025), Shivaratri (February 2026), and Holi (March 2026). Exact dates were determined by the tithi (lunar day).

**What was the Nepali fiscal year in 2082?**
