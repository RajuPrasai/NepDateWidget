## Nepal Fiscal Year in 2083

Nepal's fiscal year runs from Shrawan 1 to Ashadh end (approximately mid-July to mid-July of the following year). Within BS 2083:

- **FY 2082/83:** Shrawan 1, 2082 to Ashadh 31, 2083 - ends approximately mid-July 2026. This is the fiscal year in progress at the start of BS 2083.
- **FY 2083/84:** Shrawan 1, 2083 to Ashadh 31, 2084 - begins approximately mid-July 2026, running through mid-July 2027. Nepal's annual budget is presented and approved for this fiscal year.

All tax filings, government procurement, budget allocations, and audits in Nepal use the fiscal year notation (e.g., "FY 2083/84").
