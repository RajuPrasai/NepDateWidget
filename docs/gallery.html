Widget

Mini Bar

The collapsed bar sitting on the taskbar, showing BS date, AD date, day, and time.

Home tab

Bikram Sambat Calendar

Full BS month grid with English day numbers, tithi, public holidays, festivals, countdown banner, and the current fiscal quarter. Available in English and नेपाली.

Date tab

Nepali Date Converter – BS ↔ AD, Days & Timezone

BS ↔ AD instant conversion, add/subtract days or find the gap between two BS dates, and a two-way timezone converter with labeled UTC offsets.

Unit tab

Area & Weight Conversion

Traditional Nepali land units (Bigha, Kattha, Ropani…) and weight units (Dharni, Pawa, Mana…) side by side with metric

Text tab

Password, Word Count, Preeti ↔ Unicode, Script

Four text utilities. The Preeti ↔ Unicode converter handles both inline text and full file conversion - the most practical Nepali encoding tool.

Bank tab

Loan EMI & Multi-period Interest

Full amortization schedule tagged to BS months, and a multi-rate simple interest calculator for loans that changed rates over time.

Network tab

My IP, Ping, Scan, Trace, Whois & DNS

Six network tools in one tab. IP Scanner auto-detects your subnet, parallel-pings every host, and maps each device with hostname, MAC, and manufacturer.

More tab

Documents, Notes & Reminders

Attach local files with tags, write short notes pinned to BS dates, and set one-shot or recurring reminders. Everything lives on your machine.

RunBox

Global Launcher

Press the hotkey from anywhere in Windows to summon RunBox. Run commands, open URLs, search the web, or use quick-access shortcuts for Daraz and Maps.

Settings

Appearance, Behavior & Data Files

20 color presets across light and dark, 20 font choices, calendar toggles, RunBox hotkey, language switch, backup & restore, and direct access to all data files. Everything persists across restarts.
